## Getting Started in 5 Minutes

### 1. Navigate to Alic3X PRO

In your application, navigate to the **Alic3X** page from the main menu.

### 2. Choose Your Mode

Click the **Layout** dropdown in the header to select:
- **Solo Mode**: Single agent conversation
- **Dual Mode**: Two agents side-by-side
- **Tri Mode**: Three agents simultaneously (recommended)

### 3. Start Chatting

Type your message in the input bar at the bottom and press Enter. Your message will be sent to all active agents.

## ğŸ¯ Quick Tips

### Resizing Panels
- Hover over the thin vertical line between panels
- Click and drag left/right to adjust panel sizes
- Minimum panel width is 15% of the screen

### Switching Agents
On mobile, use the tabs at the top to switch between agents.

### Comparison View
Click the **Compare** button to see all agent responses side-by-side.

### Voice Input
1. Click the microphone icon
2. Speak your message
3. Click again to stop recording
4. Your message will be transcribed and sent
